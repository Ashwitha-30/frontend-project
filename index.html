About Karkala

Karkala is a historical town located in the Udupi District of Karnataka. Known for its rich cultural heritage, Jain temples, and scenic beauty, it attracts visitors from across the country.

Top Attractions

1. Gomateshwara Statue

A 42-foot monolithic statue of Lord Bahubali, one of the most important Jain pilgrimage sites.

2. Chaturmukha Basadi

A unique Jain temple with four identical entrances, showcasing stunning architecture.

3. Ramasamudra Lake

A serene lake built in the 15th century, offering peaceful views and a calm atmosphere.
